My Favorite Album

My favorite album currently is Between Days by Far Caspian. Ever since YouTube introduced me to this album last year I've had it on repeat. It's got very high replay value, and I can listen to it when I'm driving, working out, or just chillin in my room.

I think my favorite thing about about this music is the instrumentals. They have a lot of acoustics that are very pleasing. It gives me a sense of nostalgia when I listen to this album. The album is filled with straight bangers but my favorite song has to be Let's go outside .
